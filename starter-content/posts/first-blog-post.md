# 5 Signs Your Business Website Needs a Redesign in 2026

Your website is often the first impression customers have of your business. If it is not working hard for you, you might be losing business every day without realizing it.

## 1. It Is Not Mobile-Friendly

More than 60% of web traffic now comes from mobile devices. If your website does not look great on phones and tablets, visitors will leave within seconds.

## 2. It Is Slow to Load

A 1-second delay in page load time can reduce conversions by 7%. If your website takes more than 3 seconds to load, you are losing customers and ranking lower in Google.

## 3. It Does Not Reflect Your Current Brand

Does your website look like it was designed 5+ years ago? A dated website signals to potential customers that your business is not keeping up.

## 4. Your Conversion Rate Is Low

If visitors come to your website but do not contact you, fill out a form, or make a purchase, your website is failing at its primary job.

## 5. You Cannot Update It Yourself

If updating content requires a developer, you will never keep your website fresh. A modern CMS puts you in control.
